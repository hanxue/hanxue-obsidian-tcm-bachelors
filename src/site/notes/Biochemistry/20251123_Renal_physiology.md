Transcapillary gradient in protein concentration 跨毛细血管蛋白浓度梯度
Ditribution of fluids between plasma and extravascular spaces is influenced by osmotic pressure gradient of proteins: transcapillary gradient in protein concentration 毛细血管蛋白浓度梯度

Should be noted that protein accounts for <1% of total plasma osmolality. 血漿滲透壓

Extracellular concentration of important ions is derived from venous blood sample

Charge difference between anions and cations is called the anion gap. 
This is due to sulfate, phosphate, proteins and other organic anions not measured in clinical laoratory.

## Relevant Renal Structure

- There are 2 kidneys, each with an outer cortex 皮质 and inner medulla 髓质.
- Urine is formed inside nephron 尿液在肾单位内形成
- Each nephron contains a glomerulus 肾小球, consisting of a tuft of capillaries + afferent arterioles + efferent arterioles
- 肾小球 = 入球小动脉 + 出球小动脉 + 毛细血管
- Glomerulus is surrounded by epithelium of Bowman's capsule 包曼氏囊上皮, these structures combine to form renal corpuscle
- Initial plasma filtration happens in Bowman's capsule

- Resulting filtrate modified by secretory and reabsortive processes as it passes through the
	- proximal convoluted tubule
	- loop of Henle
	- distal convoluted tubule
	- collecting duct
- 所得滤液 被分泌以及重吸收过程，会通过：
	- 近曲小管
	- 髓袢
	- 远曲小管
	- 集合管

Renal cortex 肾皮质
- glomeruli 肾小球
- proximal, distal convoluted tubule 近曲小管、远曲小管
- loops of Henle, collecting ducts extend to medullary region
- 髓袢和集合管延伸至肾髓质
- Urine, the end product is delivered via renal pelvis 肾盂 to the ureter 输尿管, which empties into the bladder
- 尿液通过肾盂排除到输尿管，一直到膀胱

### Ultrastructural features of nephron
- Walls of each nephron is single layer of epithelial cells 肾单位壁是单层上皮细胞组成
- Podocytes in Bowman's capsule surround the glomerular capillaries and contribute to filtration barrier.
- 肾小球囊的足细胞围绕肾小球毛细血管，组成部分滤过屏障
- Cells lining convoluted tubule carry brush border of microvilli on their luminal surface and contain large numbers of intracellular mitochondria. 
- 远、近曲小管上皮细胞的腔面有一层微绒毛，包含大数量的胞内线粒体
	- Pronounced in proximal convoluted tubule, provide large surface area and rich supplies of ATP, appropriate for a region specializing in active transport.  
	- 近曲小管是主动运输区域，所以微绒毛就有很大面积而且也有丰富的ATP
	- In the descending limb of the loop of Henle, the epithelium is flat, with few organelles. Consistent with low level of active transport, and high permeability to fluid and electrolytes.  
	- 但是髓袢降支的上皮较扁平，也不多细胞器 - 较低的主动运输，较高的液体和电子渗透性
	- Cells is thick portion of ascending limb are more like those in tubules - active ion transport, very low level of permeability to water, probably because of well-developed tight juntions between adjacent epithelial cells
	- 髓袢升支粗段 细胞相似 小管细胞，拥有主动离子转运，很低的水液渗透率，相邻上皮细胞间发育良好的紧密连接

## Glomerular filtration

## Factors affecting filtration
Hydrostatic pressure > 50 mmHg 静水压 
Osmotic pressure gradient 渗透压梯度
Plasma colloid osmotic pressure 血浆胶体渗透压 25 mmHg, net filtration gradient 经滤过梯度 of 15 mmHg毫米汞柱.
In the glomerulus this results in a high filtration rate because of the very high hydraulic conductivity of the capillary wall. 
在肾小球内，15毫米汞柱导致高度过滤因为管壁有极高的导水率。
As a result, concentration of plasma proteins rises and this increases the osmotic gradient opposing filtration. 
因此血浆蛋白子浓度增加，并且对抗滤过的渗透压梯度。
Eventually osmotic gradient exactly matches the hydrostatic gradient, so no further filtration occurs. 
最终渗透压梯度和静水压平等，所以过滤也停止。


#### Interaction of forces in glomerulus
Osmotic gradient rises to balance hydrostatic gradient but never exceeds it, therefore no absorption of fluid at any point along the glomerular capillaries. 
渗透压梯度升到静水压，但是不超过静水压，所以肾小球毛细血管不发生重吸收。

### Regulation of glomerula filtration rate and renal blood flow
### 肾小球过滤率及肾血流量调节
Glomerular filtration rate = total rate of filtrations in both kidneys
肾小球过滤率 = 双肾的过滤率 ~= 120ml/min<sup>-1</sup>
Affected by these factors 被这些因素受影响：
- glomerular capillary hydrostatic pressure 肾小球毛细血管静水压
- glomerular capillary flow rate 肾小球毛细血管流速
- glomerular capillary surface area 肾小球毛细血管面积

Maintenance of **renal blood flow** is particularly important for normal filtration: main determinant of glomerular capillary pressure and flow. 
肾过滤最重要的因素是**肾血流量** , 主要决定肾小球毛细血管压和流速。

Renal blood flow is remarkably consistent at 1.2Lmin<sup>-1</sup>  despite wide range of arterial pressure: autoregulation of blood flow 
肾血流量大概1.2Lmin<sup>-1</sup> ,生理状态以及不同的动脉压情况下不变：血流量的自动调节。

arterial pressure ⬇️ -> dilation of afferent arterioles -> renal vascular resistance ⬇️ -> blood flow ⬇️
动脉压⬇️ -> 入球小动脉扩张 -> 肾血管阻力⬇️ -> 血流量 ⬇️

At <80mmHg, autoregulation breaks down and renal blood flow declines more rapidly. 
低于80毫米汞柱，肾血流量自动调节功能失效，肾血流量快速下降。

Reflex stimulation of sympathetic nerves reduce glomerular blood flow and hydrostatic pressure by constricting afferent arterioles. Therefore glomerular filtration and urine production maybe dramatically reduced in surgical shock with marked arterial hypotension. 
交感神经的反射性刺激缩小入球肾小动脉，导致腎小球血流量及靜水壓降低。
所以手术性休克伴显著动脉低血压可能导致肾小球滤过率和尿液生产大量减少。

## Modification of Glomerular Filtrate
## 肾小球滤过液的变化
- Urine is formed in two stages
	1. Filtratrion: protein-free, plasma-like fluid 300 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup>
	2. Reabsorption and secretion in nephron
		1. Hormonally controlled: volume, ionic composition and total osmolality varied according to homeostatic need. 
- 尿液形成两个步骤
	1. 过滤：无蛋白子，相似血浆液体300 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup>
	2. 肾单位重吸收以及分泌
		1. 激素根据稳态需求调节容量、离子组成、总渗透压

### Reabsorption of Na<sup>+</sup> and water
#### Proximal convoluted tubule 近曲小管
Renal transport of Na<sup>+</sup> and water are closely linked, vast bulk of fluids need to be reabsorted to avoid fatal depletion of extracellular fluids.
肾的钠离子和水分运输紧密关联：大部分水液必须重吸收否则细胞外液体致命性耗竭。

Tubules are specialized for active transport. 
肾小管专门用于主动运输。

Energy released by breakdown of ATP drives Na<sup>+</sup>  across tubular epithelium into the kidney peritubular fluid.
ATP 分解施放的能量导致钠离子透过小管上皮进入 管周组织液。
Chloride ions flow via electrostatic attraction, and the resulting osmotic gradient draws water ouf of the tubule. 
氯离子通过静电吸引渗透，渗透压梯度导致水分流出小管。
The net effect is reabsorption of NaCl solution is isosmotic with plasma, volume of filtrate is decreased but osmolality is not changed. 
氯化钠重吸收达到与血浆等渗，渗透液体积减少但是渗透压未改变。

70% of Na<sup>+</sup> is reabsorbed this way in the proximal convoluted tubule. Renal transport of this kind is referred to as gradient-time limited system. 
70%的滤过钠离子在近曲小管重吸收。叫作梯度时间限制系统。

### Loop of Henle 髓袢
In the thick ascending section of loop of Henle, active reabsoption of Na<sup>+</sup> leads to passive reabsorption of Cl<sup>-</sup>
髓袢升支粗段的钠离子主动吸收导致 氯离子被动运输。
This section is impermeable to water, therefore ion transport raises concentration of NaCl, and therefore osmolality in the medullary fluid around the whole of the loop of Henle. 
髓袢升支粗段不透水，所以离子运输导致氯化钠浓度增加，并且 包围髓袢的髓质液体渗透压也提升。

The thin section of the descending loop is permeable to ions and water, therefore fluids from the proximal convoluted tubule descends through the medulla is both concentrated and reduced in volume by passive influx of Na<sup>+</sup> and Cl<sup>-</sup>, and osmotic removal of water. 
髓袢降支细段透水，所以来自近曲小管通过髓质的离子和水分都渗透，并且浓度增加而体积减少。

Due to active removal of solute. 
当这些浓缩液体经过对面的髓袢升支，浓度清除导致浓缩度下降，液体进入远曲小管的渗透压100 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup>低于近曲小管300 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup>。
This mechanism allows for the production of hypotonic and hypertonic urine. 
这个机制可以导致低渗或高渗尿液。

Most important effect of ion transport by loop of Henle is to increase the osmolality of the intersitital fluid in the medulla of the kidney, to a maximum of 1200 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup> at the bottom of the loop. 
髓袢离子转运最重要效果是增加肾髓质的组织间渗透压，甚至在髓袢底部能达到 1200 mosmol kg<sup>-1</sup>

This provides the driving force for subsequent reabsorption of water from the collecting ducts. 
髓袢生成的渗透压是后续集合管水重吸收的动力。
